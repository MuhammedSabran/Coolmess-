About Us

At Cool Mess Family Restaurant, we believe that family time should be fun, relaxed, and delicious! Our "cool mess" concept celebrates the joyful chaos of family life, providing a space where kids can play freely while parents enjoy great food and conversation.

Founded in 2015 by the Johnson family, our restaurant has become a beloved community spot where memories are made over shared meals. We source local ingredients and prepare everything with love, just like a home-cooked meal.
